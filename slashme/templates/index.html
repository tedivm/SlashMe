Slack has gotten rid of the me command.

This app adds it back.

Privacy Policy

This application only does one thing- it listens for "slash" commands sent by the Slack server and it responds with a message. It does not have, nor does it need, permission to read any messages on your Slack network.

No data from your network or account is saved, and nothing is sent to third parties. I don't even have Google Analytics installed.
